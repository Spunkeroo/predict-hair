Hair Color Trends 2026: Top Shades & Techniques

Chicago's top colorists are seeing a major shift toward low-maintenance, lived-in color in 2026. Gone are the days of root touch-ups every 4 weeks — the hottest trends blend seamlessly into your natural color, growing out beautifully and saving you hundreds at the salon. Here are the colors dominating Chicago salons from Lincoln Park to Logan Square.

1. Expensive Brunette

The "expensive brunette" trend continues to dominate in 2026. It's a multi-dimensional brown with subtle, face-framing highlights that catch the light without looking obviously colored. Think rich chocolate with hints of caramel and toffee. Low maintenance — looks great growing out for 4-6 months. Perfect for Chicago's professional women who want polished hair without monthly salon visits. Cost at Chicago salons: $250-$400 for initial color, $150-$200 for maintenance every 12-16 weeks.

2. Copper and Auburn

Copper is having its biggest moment since the 1990s. From bright penny copper to deep auburn, red tones flatter a wide range of skin tones. Warning: copper fades faster than most colors, especially with Chicago's hard water. Use a color-depositing conditioner (like dpHUE Gloss+ in Copper, $37) between salon visits. Professional cost: $200-$350. Maintenance: every 6-8 weeks.

3. Lived-In Blonde

No more stripe-y, obvious highlights. The 2026 blonde is subtle, grown-out, and effortless. Babylights (ultra-fine highlights) create a sun-kissed effect. Shadow roots are intentional — your colorist leaves 1-2 inches of natural root for seamless grow-out. This technique is perfect for Chicago because you can go 3-4 months between appointments. Initial service: $300-$500 at Chicago salons. Maintenance: $200-$350 every 14-18 weeks.

4. Cherry Cola

A deep, warm red-brown that looks like a glass of cola held up to the light. More wearable than bright red, more interesting than standard brown. Gorgeous in Chicago's golden autumn light. Works on most hair types and skin tones. Cost: $200-$350. Maintenance: every 8-10 weeks with a clear or red-toning gloss between appointments.

Color Maintenance Tips for Chicago Water

Chicago's Lake Michigan tap water is treated with chlorine and has moderate mineral content. Both strip color faster. Solutions:

  • Shower filter: Install a vitamin C shower filter ($25-$40) to neutralize chlorine. Extends color life by 2-3 weeks
  • Color-safe shampoo: Switch to sulfate-free formulas. Pureology, Olaplex No.4, and Redken Color Extend are top picks
  • Wash less: Every 2-3 days instead of daily. Use dry shampoo between washes — Chicago humidity actually helps here since hair stays fresher longer in dry winter air
  • Cold water rinse: End showers with 30 seconds of cold water. Seals the cuticle and locks in color
